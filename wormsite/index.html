Welcome fellow, mighty mud dwellers to Wormville!


Worm Immortality Team are an indie pop outfit based in Bath and Bristol.
Fusing optimistic instrumentals with witty lyrics and a comedic outlook on
life, this 5-piece never take themselves too seriously. Having grown up
around a multitude of influences from all different genres, they bring
together all their favourite aspects of muisc in their songs.
Studying Music Technology brought them together initially, but a unique
chemistry and passion for playing music keeps the bond alive

With Ellisha on drums, Toby on bass, Nat on keys, Finn on guitar and Maisie
on vocals, they combine elements of serious musicianship with tongue-in-
cheek lyricism and vocal delivery.

Besides, who doesn't like worms?
