Ellisha's Bio

Ellisha Cox

Our mighty groove keeper Ellisha holds a precise tempo, emphases the rhythm and makes a danceable beat out of any song.

“I started learning the drums only in college, about 3 years ago. It was something I had always wanted to try out and after I learnt a few songs in music class at secondary school, and I fell in love with it. As I tend to listen to a wide variety of music, my influences can range from the psychedelic rock of Tame Impala through to chart-pop like Lady Gaga.”

Maisie's Bio

Maisie Palmer

Maisie, a.k.a dj maisie mais, a.k.a El Jefe, is in charge of all things vocal. After writing songs for years with a daisy-shaped guitar, she joined forces with Toby in order to conquer Reading town… and then the world.

“The song ‘You Suck’ will always hold a special place in my heart. From practicing it in a little box room in our Sixth Form’s music technology building, to recording the first version in an equally boxy recording studio - the song reminds me of why I make music and where it all began.”
